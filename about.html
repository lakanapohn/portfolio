About Me

I am a landscape architect and MSc student in Applied Geoinformatics.

My interests connect landscape architecture, geoinformatics, and environmental planning. I am interested in how maps, spatial data, and design can help us understand urban environments and create more resilient landscapes.

My work focuses on GIS, cartographic design, remote sensing, flood resilience, nature-based solutions, and visual communication.
