Fading Colors — Coral Reef Bleaching in Cuba

This group project explores coral reef bleaching in Cuba through cartographic storytelling. The work combines thematic mapping, visual design, and geomedia to communicate how coral reef ecosystems are affected by environmental stress.

The project includes an ArcGIS StoryMap and printed map materials designed to present the topic clearly for a wider audience.
